FITNESS

Balance provides you with a simple guide to what we think is a good work life balance to stay healthy! We think it is important to stay active because it keeps your mood up and ready to tackle your next task!
Here are a couple of weekly schedules to keep you active!

EX1: Lifting Schedule

  • Monday

    Start off easy! Monday calls for CHEST DAY Here are a couple of our favorite workouts:
    1. Bench press (4 sets of 8)
    2. Incline bench press (A bit harder to balance) (4 sets of 6)
    3. Push ups (Nice and EZ!) (4 sets of 15)
    4. Dips (4 sets of 10)

  • Tuesday

    Take a break from upper body, time for LEGS:
    1. Squats! (4 sets of 8)
    2. Good mornings (4 sets of 12)
    3. RDLs (3 sets of 8)
    4. Hip abduction * hip aduction (3 sets of 10)

  • Wednesday

    TAKE A REST DAY! Whether it be the midddle of the week, Sunday, or just your favorite day to relax, rest days are always important and gives your muscles time to grow AND relax!

  • Thursday

    Back to upper body, BACK DAY Here are a couple of work outs:
    1. Seated cable row (4 sets of 8)
    2. Barbell rows (4 sets of 8)
    3. PULL UPS! (Hard to master but a great back workout! Do as many as you can)
    4. Lat pull down (4 sets of 10)

  • Friday

    Use this day to double up on a muscle you want to focus on, do another leg day, chest day, maybe even just glutes day ;)

  • Saturday

    Saturday calls for ARM DAY:
    1. Bicep curls (4 sets of 8)
    2. Tricep kickbacks (3 sets of 10)
    3. Overhead extensino (4 sets of 8)
    4. Palms down wrist curls (3 sets of 6)

  • Sunday

    SHOULDER DAY!
    1. Barbell shoulder press (4 sets of 8)
    2. Lateral raise (4 sets of 8)
    3. Dumbbell front raise (3 sets of 10)
    4. Arnold press (4 sets of 10 )
